SSR - A Photon in a double slit!

Young's double slit experiment, particle-wave duality of Photon, and Sushant Singh Rajput.


One who embraces the realm of quantum mechanics, and the ever mystifying, mind boggling truth of dual nature of Photon, would find this short-tribute reflecting all which he essentially meant about his life.


Besides being a wonderful actor, he was in love with astronomy. A person who could talk for hours about astrophysics, quantum mechanics and cosmology with equal interest as about the world of cinema. A man who would jump with equal excitement for a new film project, as he would do on witnessing the statue of Nataraja on his visit to CERN - a hub of complex particle accelerators.

Gazing stars with his telescope like a curious kid, an avid reader and a crazy lord shiva fanatic. Sushant Singh Rajput - A true polymath

A creative projection of photon's duality blends well with his exceptional life.
